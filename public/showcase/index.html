Medical Benefits

Medical plans are administered by Aetna®. Care received within the RWJBarnabas Health system is completely free* across all plans. Choose the plan that fits where you live and how you use care.

★ Care at RWJBarnabas Health = $0 Cost to You

Across all plans, in-network Premier Tier (RWJBH facilities, employed doctors, Rutgers Health) — deductible $0, coinsurance 0%, $0 copay. *Payroll deductions and $200 ER copay still apply.

⚠️ Use It or Lose It — Important FSA Rules

Healthcare FSA: You may roll over up to $680 into 2027. Dependent Care FSA: No rollover — plan contributions carefully. Changes only allowed during Annual Enrollment or after a Qualifying Life Event.
